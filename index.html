Spotlight Project

AI System for Osteoarthritis Diagnosis

🏥 California Rheumatology Clinic Dataset  ·  📅 January 2025 – April 2025  ·  4 Classes: CONTROL · OA · RA · RA+OA
Abstract: Osteoarthritis (OA) is a degenerative joint disease that increasingly burdens aging populations. This study explores AI-enabled, image-based diagnostic approaches for hand OA by leveraging high-resolution hand and wrist images captured via smartphone technology. Using convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for image analysis alongside questionnaire data, we propose a multimodal framework that extracts and fuses features from visual and structured information to enhance diagnostic accuracy and enable earlier, personalized management of hand OA. The methodology encompasses image preprocessing, CNN architecture design, feature extraction, and several fusion strategies (early, late, Joint Probability Function) with attention mechanisms and ensemble learning. Findings on a multi-category dataset (4 classes) demonstrate the potential of AI-driven, accessible diagnostic tools to reduce time and resource demands compared with conventional practices.
